# Email Sequence Generation Prompt

## SEQUENCE TYPES
1. **Welcome Sequence** (5 emails, new subscriber → course awareness)
2. **Course Launch** (7 emails, awareness → cart open → urgency)
3. **Weekly Newsletter** (1 email, seasonal tip + student spotlight)
4. **Re-engagement** (3 emails, win back cold subscribers)

## EMAIL STRUCTURE
1. **Subject Line** (Curiosity or benefit. 40 chars max)
2. **Preview Text** (Expands on subject, 80 chars max)
3. **Opening** (Personal greeting, 1-2 sentences max)
4. **Story/Teaching** (300-500 words, scannable with subheads)
5. **Transition** ("Here's why I'm telling you this...")
6. **Offer/CTA** (One clear action — download checklist, watch training, join course)
7. **P.S.** (Bonus thought, urgency, or personal note)

## TONE CHECKLIST
- [ ] Sounds like a letter from a capable friend, not a corporation
- [ ] Uses "you" and "your" more than "I" and "my"
- [ ] Includes one specific detail (season, harvest, kitchen moment)
- [ ] Ends with capability, never fear
- [ ] CTA feels like an invitation to learn, not a demand to buy
