# GENERATECONTENT Command

Check Notion Content Calendar for Status = 'Draft' and Assigned To = 'Claude'. Generate content using prompts from GitHub repo (02-CONTENT-ENGINE/prompts/). Save output to 02-CONTENT-ENGINE/output/drafts/. Update Notion status to 'Review'. Consider seasonal relevance.

## Content Types & Prompts

### Blog Post
- 1,500–2,000 words
- Tone: Warm, capable, grandmother's kitchen energy, never fear-mongering
- Include student testimonial where available
- End with FAQ schema block
- Soft CTA to course or free checklist
- SEO keyword in H1, first paragraph, and 2–3 subheadings

### Social Media (7-post weekly plan)
- Monday: Motivation/Quote
- Tuesday: Quick Tip
- Wednesday: Student Story
- Thursday: Before/After
- Friday: Free Resource
- Saturday: Community Spotlight
- Sunday: Sabbath Reflection

### Email Sequence
- Story-based opening
- One practical tip or insight per email
- Soft CTA in emails 1–4, direct offer in email 5
- Warm sign-off from Ingrid

### Sales Page
- Forbes Riley method: Hook → Story → Demo → Emotion → Single CTA
- Highlight $147 one-time, lifetime access
- Include course breakdown (Sustain/Preserve/Heal/Renew)
- 14-day first-module guarantee
