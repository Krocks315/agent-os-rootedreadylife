# Weekly Report Command
**Trigger:** WEEKLYREPORT

## Instructions
Check the Notion databases (Content Calendar, Backlink Tracker, AI Citation Monitor) and fill out the Weekly Report Template. Identify what was accomplished, what's blocked, and what the next week's priorities should be. Generate any missing content for the upcoming week.

## Steps
1. Open Notion "Agent OS - RootedReadyLife"
2. Check Content Calendar for items with Status = "Published" this week
3. Check Backlink Tracker for new links acquired
4. Check AI Citation Monitor for schema progress
5. Fill out the Weekly Report Template
6. Identify next week's top 3 priorities
7. Generate content for items with Status = "Draft" and Publish Date within next 7 days

## Seasonal Focus
Always consider the season when generating content:
- **Spring (Mar–May):** Planting, seed starting, spring herbs
- **Summer (Jun–Aug):** Harvest, canning, preserving, peak garden
- **Fall (Sep–Nov):** Root cellaring, late harvest, winter prep
- **Winter (Dec–Feb):** Planning, seed ordering, indoor herbs, juicing
